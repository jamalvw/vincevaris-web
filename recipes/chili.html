chili

this stuff is good with rice i promise

ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef or ground whatever
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 14.5oz (small) can of petite diced tomatoes
  • 29oz (big) can of tomato sauce
  • 15.5oz (small) can of dark red kidney beans
  • chili seasoning
    • 2 tbsps chili powder
    • 1 tsp ground cumin
    • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
    • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/4 tsp black ground pepper

steps:

  1. brown some beef on medium-high in a pot with a little bit of oil. i like to do everything in one pot, but it's up to you. break up the meat occasionally with a big spoon or something
  2. dice a yellow onion and a green bell pepper, then add it to the pot with teaspoon of salt. add a tiny bit of water if something seems like it's burning. you shouldn't have to though
  3. lower the heat to low, then pour in a small can of petite diced tomatoes and a big can of tomato sauce.
  4. add chili seasoning. you can just get a packet of this stuff at a grocery store, otherwise toss in 2 tbsps chili powder, 1 tsp ground cumin, 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp black ground pepper. stir that all up
  5. drain a small can of dark red kidney beans and pour it in
  6. let cook on low for about 1 hour or until thick. enjoy. i recommend putting this with buttered rice it is insanely good
